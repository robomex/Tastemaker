Grand Opens is a simple, up-to-date iOS app that’s your guide to the newest food & booze around you.

Be bold and check out a new bar opening today.
See what people are saying about that fancy restaurant that opened last week.
Discuss what you loved (or hated) about your latest foodie adventure.


Grand Opens is about adventure.
Not the adventure that takes place on the side of a mountain – the adventure that takes place with best friends, 10 minutes from home, after a long week of work.



Grand Opens is built in Chicago by Tony. Let me know of any feedback or questions via email or Twitter.
